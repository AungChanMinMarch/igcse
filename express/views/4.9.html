Power

The output power of a machine is the rate at which it does work.

The output power of a machine is the same as the rate at which it transfers useful energy.

The useful energy transferred is equal to the work done.

Equation of Power

$\text{power P (in watts)} = \frac{\text{work done (in joules)}}{\text{time taken (in seconds)}}$

$\text{power P (in watts)} = \frac{\text{useful energy transferred (in joules)}}{\text{time taken (in seconds)}}$

1 kilowatt (kW)= $10^3$ watts

1 megawatt (MW) = $10^6$ watts
