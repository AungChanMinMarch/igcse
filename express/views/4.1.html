Energy can be stored in objects in different ways and transferred in different ways.

Energy transferred to an object can be stored in different ways to the ways in which it was stored before being transferred.

Kinetic Energy

Kinetic Energy is the energy of an object due to its motion.

Gravitational Potential Energy

Gravitational potential energy is the energy of an object dut to its position.

Chemical Energy

Chemical energy is the energy stored in a substance and released when chemical reactions take place.

Internal Energy

Internal Energy is the energy of an object due to the internal motion and positions of its molecules

Nuclear Energy

Nuclear Energy is the energy released when the nucleus of an atom splits or disintegrates.

Electrical energy

Electrical energy is energy transferred by an electric current.

Thermal energy

The thermal energy is the energy tranfer from a hot object to a cold object.

Sound Energy

Sound Energy is the energy transfer by sound waves.

light Energy

Light energy is the energy transfer by light.

Work done

Work done is the energy transfer by a force or by electricity.
