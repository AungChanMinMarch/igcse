Work done

The work you do causes transfer of energy.

The work done by a force depends on the force and the distance moved.
It is defined as the product of the force (in newtons) and the distance moved in the direction of the force (in metres).

gravitational potential energy

In general, when an object of mass m is raised or lowered by a vertical distance $h$,
its change of gravitational potential energy = $mgh$

equation of kinetic energy

$$ \begin{aligned} \text{kinetic energy} & = \frac{1}{2}mv^2 \\ \text{where, } m & = \text{the mass of the object in kilograms} \\ v & = \text{the speed of the object in } m/s \\ \end{aligned} $$
